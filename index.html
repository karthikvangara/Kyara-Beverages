Citrus Iced Tea

Cool, refreshing, and zesty!

A bold black tea served chilled with fresh lime slices, mint leaves, and ice cubes. This invigorating beverage is perfect for sunny days and complements any meal with its crisp, citrusy kick.

Mint Lime Sparkle

Freshness in every sip!

A vibrant blend of lime wedges, crushed mint, and sparkling water over ice. This detox-inspired drink is crisp, clean, and naturally hydrating—ideal for health-conscious refreshment.

Oreo Milkshake

Dessert in a glass!

A creamy, indulgent blend of vanilla ice cream, crushed Oreos, and chocolate drizzle, topped with whipped cream and served with a classic striped straw. A perfect sweet treat to chill out.
